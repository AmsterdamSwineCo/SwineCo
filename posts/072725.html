MtG Cube Draft: Wednesday Nights

Join us in the cellar Wednesdays at 6p to draft Ze Cube and play three 50-min. rounds, teams at 6 & 8 players, free-for-all at 5 & 7 players

Watch Luis Scott-Vargas (LSV) and Andrew Baeckstrom (BK) break down the top 50 cards to keep an eye out for while you draft and explain how they’re best employed. Take them highly, play them often and consider who you’re passing them to in a team draft.
